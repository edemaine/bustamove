Bust-a-Move

by Erik Demaine and Stefan Langerman, 2015

An implementation of this paper / NP-hardness proof

Gadgets

  1. Split
  2. OR
  3. AND
  4. Crossover
  5. Big, correct padding, but not enough
  6. Big, full padding, but blanks instead of bubbles

Instructions

  • Aim with left/right arrow keys. Hold shift to move slowly.
  • Shoot with spacebar.
  • Use A/S/D to aim at 45/90/45 degrees.
  • Ball sequence is shown at the bottom.
  • Use Z to toggle more/less zoom views (for larger constructions).
